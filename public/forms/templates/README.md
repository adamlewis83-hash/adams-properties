# Forms Library — Oregon residential leasing templates

Drop your blank Oregon residential leasing forms here as PDFs.
Once placed, point Claude at this folder and the app will wire them
up to a Document Library that fills in tenant data and emails to
tenants on demand.

## Where to put what

```
public/forms/templates/
├── pre-lease/                 ← before tenant moves in
│   ├── rental-application.pdf
│   └── tenant-screening-criteria.pdf
│
├── move-in/                   ← signed at move-in
│   ├── pet-addendum.pdf
│   ├── smoking-addendum.pdf
│   ├── mold-disclosure.pdf
│   ├── lead-paint-disclosure.pdf
│   ├── lead-paint-epa-pamphlet.pdf
│   ├── fair-housing-pamphlet.pdf
│   └── receipt-move-in-funds.pdf
│
├── during-tenancy/            ← issued during the lease term
│   ├── 24hr-entry-notice.pdf
│   ├── repair-request-acknowledgment.pdf
│   └── maintenance-work-order.pdf
│
├── late-default/              ← when rent is late or for-cause
│   └── (72hr / 144hr / 30-day notices already programmatic — see Notices card on lease page)
│
├── renewal/                   ← lease renewal / rent change
│   ├── renewal-letter.pdf
│   └── 90-day-rent-increase-notice.pdf
│
└── move-out/                  ← when the tenant leaves
    ├── security-deposit-accounting.pdf
    └── abandonment-notice.pdf
```

## Notes

- **Filenames matter** — keep them lowercase with hyphens, no spaces. The app will use them as IDs.
- **Form fields are a bonus.** If your PDF is an Acrobat-fillable form (AcroForm), the app can auto-fill tenant name, address, dates, etc. If it's a flat scan, the app will just let you download or send it as-is.
- **One PDF per form** — don't combine the lease + addendum + disclosure into one file. Keep them separate so they can be sent individually.
- **Don't put filled-in/signed copies here.** Those go through the per-tenant Documents card on each lease detail page (Supabase Storage, access-controlled).
- **Files in this folder are publicly viewable** at `https://adams-properties.vercel.app/forms/templates/<category>/<filename>.pdf`. That's intentional — tenants need to be able to view blank forms during signing flows. Blank templates are fine to be public; only filled copies with PII need to be locked down.

## Already programmatic (no PDF needed)

These are generated by the app from your lease/property data — no template needed:

- Residential Lease Agreement (Oregon ORS Chapter 90 compliant)
- Move-in Condition Report
- Move-out Condition Report
- 72-hour Pay-or-Vacate Notice (ORS 90.394)
- 144-hour Pay-or-Vacate Notice (ORS 90.394)
- 30-day For-Cause Notice (ORS 90.392)
- Rent Ledger
- Signature Certificate (when in-app E-Sig was used)
